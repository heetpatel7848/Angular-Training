Read Book With US

The Psychology of Money

by Morgan Housel

Content

It covers observations on our relationship with money and tells us how our thinking towards finances drives the critical decisions of our life. The premise of this book is that - doing well with money has a little to do with how smart you are and a lot to do with how you behave.

Rich Dad Poor Dad

by Robert T. Kiyosaki and Sharon Lechter

Content

It advocates the importance of financial literacy (financial education), financial independence and building wealth through investing in assets, real estate investing, starting and owning businesses, as well as increasing one's financial intelligence (financial IQ).

Do Eoic Shit

by Ankur Warikoo

Content

It advocates the importance of financial literacy (financial education), financial independence and building wealth through investing in assets, real estate investing, starting and owning businesses, as well as increasing one's financial intelligence (financial IQ).

The Power of Your Subconscious Mind

by Joseph Murphy

Content

The central point of the book is that you can use your imagination to suggest ideas to your subconscious to get what you want. If repeated often enough, your mind will then steer your behavior more towards making those ideas a reality, unbeknownst to you.

Ikigai

by Francesc Miralles and Hector Garcia

Content

Ikigai is a Japanese concept that means your 'reason for being. ' 'Iki' in Japanese means 'life,' and 'gai' describes value or worth. Your ikigai is your life purpose or your bliss. It's what brings you joy and inspires you to get out of bed every day.

How to Win Friends and Influence People

by Dale Carnegie

Content

The core idea is that you can change other people's behavior simply by changing your own. It teaches you the principles to better understand people, become a more likable person, improve relationships, win others over, and influence behavior through leadership.
